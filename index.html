Welcome to Kijabe Hospital

About Us

Kijabe Hospital being one of the profound hospitals in the country was started by Dr.Albert Gikonyo. He was one of the foreigners who came during the time Kenya had not gained indipendence. He saw the disaster which struck upon our citizens and he collaborated with the late Kenyatta where he aimed to cure and provide health to the republicants. Today Kijabe Hospital is one of the most famous hospitals not only in Kenya but in Africa

Our Mission

To provide quality health service to Africans and generate a better future free from Diseases #Cure for All

Our Vision

Raising the next healthier generation
